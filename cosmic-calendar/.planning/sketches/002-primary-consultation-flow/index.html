ח

Letter Chet · Reuben · sight

An ancient map of the month.

Cosmic Calendar uses the Gra / Arizal-Gra attribution lineage as its working spine. In that tradition, every Hebrew month is connected to a zodiac sign, a tribe, a Hebrew letter, and a human faculty.

For Tammuz, those links are Cancer, Reuben, Chet, and sight. The app translates that source language into an invitation to see clearly rather than a prediction about what will happen.

Source note: Sefer Yetzirah, Gra Version, chapter 5, cross-checked through the project's GalEinai research. Traditions and recensions vary.
