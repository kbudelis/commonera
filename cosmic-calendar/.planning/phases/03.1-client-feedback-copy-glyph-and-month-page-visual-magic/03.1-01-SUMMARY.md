# Phase 03.1 Plan 1: Client Feedback Summary

**The client-feedback direction is implemented as a shorter welcome, a more
specific personal portrait system, and an explorable twelve-month celestial
reading gallery.**

## Accomplishments

- Reduced the welcome from four pages to the approved two-page sequence.
- Added one authored narrative, Light, Shadow Wisdom, and question for every
  normalized birth month.
- Made the personal zodiac visual an accessible edit/re-entry control.
- Replaced the single constellation presentation with a circular twelve-month
  gallery supporting pointer, click, and keyboard navigation.
- Reworked the post-birth frames as complete screen-sized compositions and
  preserved ritual/source grounding.
- Expanded flow/content/CSS regression coverage around the accepted changes.

## Deviations and scope evolution

- The old Phase 1 welcome-level skip and proximity-only settling were
  intentionally superseded: the birthday step owns the skip, and the post-birth
  reading stack settles one full screen at a time.
- The old shared Personal Thread presentation was intentionally superseded by
  twelve authored portraits while retaining the stable derived-month model.
- Browse-all-twelve moved forward from future scope because the gallery became
  the chosen static exploration treatment before rotor integration.
