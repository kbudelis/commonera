01

Registers

Three registers, not themes — day, dusk, night — and every screen declares one. The descent moves through them in order; havdalah runs them in reverse with a gold bias. Grain descends with the palette: heaviest and multiplied on parchment, faint and screen-blended by night. The crossing is layered gradients, never a flat color-mix.

02

Color

The contract palette, unchanged. Below it, the ink washes — printed tints derived from contract colors by recipe (proposed amendment A1), for day-register accents only. They behave like a letterpress second color, never a glowing gradient. And gold behaves like light, not color: every gold element carries an ember glow.

03

Type

Stamp plus book. OTR No Cigar — unicase, wide-tracked, letterpress — is display and label only; it never sets a sentence. Newsreader carries every sentence; it never sets a timestamp. Within a serif line, the load-bearing word takes italic — never bold, never gold, never underline. One italic phrase per line, at most.
